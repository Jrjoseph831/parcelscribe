Use this when your claim was denied and you need to restate the facts with clearer proof.

**Template**

Subject: Appeal for Tracking {{trackingNumber}}

Hello,

I am appealing the denial of claim for tracking {{trackingNumber}}. Shipment date {{shipDate}}; expected {{expectedDelivery}}.

Summary: {{briefIssue}}
Key evidence attached:
- Proof of shipment
- Proof of value
- Photos (if damaged)
- Tracking timeline showing last scan

Request: Reconsider and approve reimbursement.

Thank you,
{{yourName}}
{{contactInfo}}
