Use this letter when submitting a claim. Keep it short and reference attachments.

**Template**

Subject: Claim for Tracking {{trackingNumber}}

Hello,

I am filing a claim for tracking {{trackingNumber}} shipped on {{shipDate}}. The package was expected {{expectedDelivery}}.

What happened: {{briefIssue}}

Attachments:
- Proof of shipment
- Proof of value
- Photos (if damaged)
- Tracking timeline

Request: Reimburse the item value.

Thank you,
{{yourName}}
{{contactInfo}}
