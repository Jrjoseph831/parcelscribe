## What you need
- Tracking number
- Proof of shipment (label/drop receipt)
- Proof of value
- Photos of exterior and interior damage

## Steps
1. Photograph exterior box, interior packaging, and damaged item.
2. Compile tracking timeline and value documents.
3. File the claim online with photos and proof of value.
4. Respond to USPS follow-ups quickly; schedule inspection if requested.

## Common mistakes
- No interior packaging photos
- Missing proof of value
- Narrative is too long
- Waiting for USPS to contact you before submitting

## What to upload
- Photos: exterior, interior, damaged item
- Proof of value
- Proof of shipment

## Example claim narrative
"Item arrived damaged; photos of exterior, interior packaging, and item attached. Proof of value and shipment provided. Request reimbursement for the contents."
