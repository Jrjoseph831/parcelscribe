## What you need
- Tracking number and last scan
- Proof of shipment (label/drop receipt)
- Proof of value
- Recipient contact details

## Steps
1. Confirm last scan and expected delivery date.
2. Gather proof of value and shipment.
3. Submit the missing mail search; then file the claim with attachments.
4. Respond to USPS follow-ups within 24 hours.
5. Appeal with clearer evidence if denied.

## Common mistakes
- Filing without proof of value
- Long narratives instead of bullets
- Skipping the missing mail search
- Waiting weeks before filing

## What to upload
- Proof of shipment
- Proof of value
- Tracking timeline with last scan

## Example claim narrative
"Tracking XXXX last scanned 01/02; expected delivery 01/04. Proof of shipment and value attached. Filing missing mail search and claim. Request reimbursement for the contents."
