## What you need
- Tracking number and last scan
- Proof of shipment (label or drop receipt)
- Proof of value (invoice/receipt)
- Recipient contact details

## Steps
1. Gather proof of shipment and proof of value.
2. List last scan, expected delivery date, and who is filing (shipper/recipient).
3. Submit the claim with attachments and a brief reimbursement request.
4. Respond to any FedEx requests within 24 hours.
5. Appeal with clearer evidence if denied.

## Common mistakes
- Waiting for more scans instead of filing
- No proof of value
- Submitting long paragraphs instead of bullets
- Leaving out contact details

## What to upload
- Proof of shipment
- Proof of value
- Tracking timeline with last scan

## Example claim narrative
"Tracking XXXX last scanned 01/02; expected delivery 01/04. Proof of shipment and value attached. Filing as shipper; recipient contact provided. Request reimbursement for the contents."
